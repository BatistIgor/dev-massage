Massage Therapy for Body & Soul

Every session is a step towards harmony, let me be your guide.

About me

My name is Daria. I am a professional with 15 years of experience in massage therapy, a specialist in Renata Franka's lymphatic drainage technique and a certified osteopath with over 10,000 hours of practice.

My main goal is to help each client find harmony of body and mind using an individualized approach and a combination of different techniques, from lymphatic drainage and craniosacral therapy to visceral massage.

I believe that the hands of a master can not only relieve pain and stress, but also restore vital energy, create comfort and confidence in your body. For me, this is not just a job – it is an art that I am perfecting every day.
